# Research-as-a-Service — Base76 Research Intelligence

> *Deep epistemic research — with confidence scores.*

---

## What is this?

Not consulting. Not a literature review. Not ChatGPT with a prompt.

**Research-as-a-Service** delivers **epistemic briefs** — structured answers to complex questions, with explicit confidence scores, identified knowledge gaps, and traceable reasoning. Every claim is graded. Every gap is named. Every source is evaluated, not just cited.

You get clarity on what the research actually says, where knowledge ends, and what to do next.

---

## Who is this for?

**AI companies building regulated products**
You need to understand a domain fast — not a summary, but an honest account of what is known, contested, and unknown. Example: *"Can we use a 7B LLM in a clinical workflow under the EU AI Act?"*

**Policy and compliance organizations**
The EU AI Act (effective August 2026) requires AI systems in high-risk domains to be interpretable and auditable. We produce epistemic audits that map exactly what your system can — and cannot — reliably claim to know.

**Researchers without research time**
You need a literature landscape, gap analysis, or replication critique — done rigorously, not generically. Co-authorship is an accepted form of payment for academic engagements.

---

## What you get: The Epistemic Brief

A brief is a structured epistemic artifact, not a report. It delivers:

1. **Question analysis** — what the question actually asks (≠ what you thought you were asking)
2. **Knowledge landscape** — what is established, what is contested, what is unknown
3. **Source evaluation** — epistemic confidence per source, not just citation count
4. **Mechanistic analysis** — *why* things are the way they are, not just *what* they are
5. **Recommendation with verdict** — PASS / REFINE / ESCALATE / BLOCK + confidence score
6. **Traceable source chain** — powered by [CognOS proof-engine](https://github.com/base76-research-lab/cognos-proof-engine)

Delivered in: **Markdown + interactive HTML + PDF**
Timeline: **48–72 hours** from brief received

## Why Base76?

**Epistemic honesty is non-negotiable.**
Every brief ships with explicit confidence scores. We tell you what we know, what we don't know — and what it costs not to find out. No simulated certainty.

**Mechanistic depth, not surface analysis.**
Base76's research background in [mechanistic interpretability](https://github.com/base76-research-lab/Mechanistic-Interpretability) means we understand *how* AI systems work internally — not just what they output. This matters when you need to explain a system under regulatory scrutiny.

**CognOS — open-source epistemic infrastructure.**
Our [CognOS proof-engine](https://github.com/base76-research-lab/cognos-proof-engine) generates audit trails for every claim in a brief. This is the same infrastructure used internally for confidence scoring and is available as open source.
